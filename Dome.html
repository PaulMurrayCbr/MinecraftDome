This page allows you to generate dome shapes for minecraft. Sorry about the UI being a bit rough.

  1. Add a layer.
  2. Draw your support blocks on the layer (eg: a circle, two or three points).
  3. (optional) Adjust the anchors to change the height of your layer, or to give it a slope.
  4. On the surface mask, draw in a shape covering your supports.

Coordinates are minecraft coordinates. 0,0 is at the top-left of the screen. Top of the screen is North (negative Z). Right of the screen is East (positive X). Elevation is Y.

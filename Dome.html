This page allows you to generate dome shapes for minecraft. Sorry about the UI being a bit rough.

Add a layer. Draw your anchor blocks on the layer (for a dome, this will be a circle).

Sometimes you have to wiggle the resize handle on the mask layer to make it draw properly. I don't know why.
